Shipping to Individuals

You asked and we listened! With the increase in remote work and online learning, many organizations have a need for books to be shipped to individual recipients. Our individual fulfillment services allow you to do just that.

To get started: If you have your recipient addresses, download and fill out our Mail Innovations Address Template . If you need help collecting addresses, let us know and we can set up an address collection landing page for your organization (additional charges apply). All orders include a light address verification for your convenience.
